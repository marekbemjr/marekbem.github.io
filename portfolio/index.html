Experience

My technical expertise includes tools such as SQL, Excel, Python, and Salesforce, which I use to deliver innovative, data-driven solutions.

About Me

From a young age, I've been captivated by the stories data can tell. This passion led me to a career where I transform complex datasets into actionable business strategies.
